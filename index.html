Welcome to Our Institute

The International Plant Machinery Institute is dedicated to providing high-quality technical education and hands-on training in heavy plant operations, maintenance, and safety. Our goal is to empower skilled professionals for the growing machinery industry worldwide.

Courses Offered:

  • Excavator Operation and Maintenance
  • Bulldozer Handling and Safety
  • Crane Rigging and Lifting
  • Hydraulic Systems Engineering
  • Plant Machinery Management
